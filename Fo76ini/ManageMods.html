FAQ

How to add mods?
Click on the green plus button or drag and drop them into the mod order.

How to completely disable mods?
Check "Disable mods" and click on "Deploy". All mods will be removed.
The sResourceIndexFileList will temporarly be saved in QuickConfiguration.ini instead of the Fallout76Custom.ini, so you don't lose your manually installed mods.

What happens to mods I've installed manually?
The tool won't touch these mods. If you want to manage them, you can import them: File > Import installed mods

Why are you distributing Archive2 with your tool after all, even though you initially said you wouldn't?
I changed my mind.


Known issues

bundled_textures.ba2 is missing
Please look into archive2.log.txt.
If you can spot "System.AggregateException", then one or more of your mods have corrupted *.dds files.
Use "Tools > Repair all *.dds files" to repair ALL *.dds files.
OR
Select a mod, click on the pen to edit mod details, and click on "Repair *.dds files".
NOTE: This might take a while, so grab a coffee or a snack... or both.

Sound mods don't work
If your sound mods don't work, e.g. Appalachia Warfare, try the following settings:

  • Install as: Separate *.ba2 archive
  • Archive format: General
  • Uncheck "Compress archive"
